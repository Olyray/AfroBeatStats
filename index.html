About AfroBeatStats

I got the idea for this app after the week that we treated APIs at ALX. One day, I was using Spotify and I thought to myself, who is the top artist in Nigeria based on statistics?

I couldn't find any tool online that answered the question, and so, AfroBeatStats was born.
